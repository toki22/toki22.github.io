Jump-throw Bind

For consistent jump throws, use one of the following (replace n with any key):


1) One-Key Jump Throw

In your config:

    alias "+jumpthrow" "+jump;-attack"
    alias "-jumpthrow" "-jump"
    bind "n" "+jumpthrow"

In game:

  • hold down mouse1 with your grenade
  • press "n"


1) Two-Key Jump Throw

In your config:

    bind "n" "-attack"

In game:

  • hold down mouse1 with your grenade
  • press spacebar and "n" simultaneously using your thumb


Large Crosshair Bind

To easily line up grenades, use one of the following (replace "v" with any key):


1) Hold to Enable Large Crosshair

In your config: [replace the third line with what your current corsshair settings are]

    bind "v" "+nadexhair"
    alias "+nadexhair" "cl_crosshairgap -10; cl_crosshairsize 5000"
    alias "-nadexhair" "cl_crosshairgap -1; cl_crosshairsize 4"

In game:

  • hold down "v" to enable the large crosshair
  • letting go of "v" will disable the large crosshair


2) Toggle Large Crosshair

In your config: [replace the third line with what your current crosshair settings are]

    bind "v" "nadexon"
    alias "nadexon" "cl_crosshairgap -10; cl_crosshairsize 5000; bind v nadexoff"
    alias "nadexoff" "cl_crosshairgap -1; cl_crosshairsize 4; bind v nadexon"

In game:

  • press "v" to enable the large crosshair
  • press "v" a second time to diable the large crosshair
